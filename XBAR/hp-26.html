CrossBar Memory Solutions:
Cramium + ReRAM

CrossBar provides advanced memory and security solutions tailored for AI, IoT, and decentralized systems. Our patented ReRAM technology enables terabyte-scale storage with significantly reduced power consumption.

As our latest advacement, the Cramium PHSM integrates Multi-Party Computation (MPC) within a secure element to eliminate key reconstruction and reduce attack surfaces. With built-in FIDO2 authentication and an offline-by-default architecture, it provides reliable, high-assurance protection for digital assets.

Cramium Overview Explore Technology

Revolutionary Memory Technology

CrossBar's ReRAM technology delivers unprecedented performance, density, and power efficiency for next-generation computing applications.

High Density

Terabyte-scale on-chip storage with minimal layout overhead. Ultra-dense, stackable 3D cross-point arrays.

Energy Efficient

5× less energy than eFlash, 20× less than NAND, 30× less than SPI Flash, and 40× less than BLE.

High Performance

20ns read, 12μs write, no erase latency. 100× faster reads, and 1,000× faster writes than traditional memory.

Enhanced Security

Tamper-resistant storage for cryptographic keys. Prevents extraction and supports secure keys.

ReRAM In Action

Designed to bridge embedded and storage-class memory needs, aligning with the most demanding future chip architectures.
