Explore Technology Cramium Overview

Revolutionary Memory Technology

CrossBar's ReRAM technology delivers unprecedented performance, density, and power efficiency for next-generation computing applications.

High Density

Terabyte-scale on-chip storage with minimal layout overhead. Ultra-dense, stackable 3D cross-point arrays.

Energy Efficient

5× less energy than eFlash, 20× less than NAND, 30× less than SPI Flash, and 40× less than BLE.

High Performance

20ns read, 12μs write, no erase latency. 100× faster reads, and 1,000× faster writes than traditional memory.

Enhanced Security

Tamper-resistant storage for cryptographic keys. Prevents extraction and supports secure keys.

Cramium Product Family

Our innovative ReRAM-based products designed for the most demanding applications.

Cramium PHSM

Physically secure hardware security module for cryptographic key storage and secure boot.

  • Capacity: 1-8Mb
  • Read Speed: 20ns
  • Write Cycles: 1M+
  • Security: Tamper-resistant

Cramium HPM

High-performance memory for AI accelerators and edge computing applications.

  • Capacity: 128Mb-1Gb
  • Read Speed: 15ns
  • Power: 0.5pJ/bit
  • Interface: LPDDR5

High-density memory for storage-class applications and large-scale data processing.

  • Capacity: 4-64Gb

  • Endurance: 100K cycles
  • Interface: PCIe 5.0
